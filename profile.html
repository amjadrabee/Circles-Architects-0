About Circles

An engineering consulting office that provides architectural and interior design services that are based on the careful study of a team of highly experienced professional architects, engineers and designers.

We aim for quality in work, constant creativity and achieving the highest possible comfort for users by carefully studying the needs of the project and finding the optimal solutions to reach comprehensive sustainability, and our keenness on the element of beauty and creativity in our designs is what makes our work the focus of attention and admiration.

We always strive to update our knowledge to be fully aware of all that is new in terms of modern construction and new materials and to keep pace with the markets, and we work on the continuous development of our work tools to maintain the quality of the work and the accuracy and efficiency of the products.
